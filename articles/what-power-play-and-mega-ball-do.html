What Power Play and Mega Ball actually do

Bonus fields are often the most confusing part of a results page. Clear explanations help users understand what they are looking at without needing to leave the site.

Quick answer

Powerball and Mega Ball are special draw numbers, while Power Play is a multiplier field that changes certain prize outcomes. Good results pages separate these clearly and label them correctly.

Why these fields matter

On draw pages, visitors are not only checking the base number set. They are also looking for bonus numbers, multiplier fields, and any game-specific format that changes the prize structure.

Powerball and Power Play

The Powerball is the special ball in the draw result. Power Play is a multiplier field that can affect certain non-jackpot prize levels, which is why it deserves separate display in the result cards.

Mega Millions and Mega Ball

Mega Ball works similarly as a clearly separated special number in Mega Millions. If that field is hidden or poorly labeled, the result presentation feels incomplete.

How results are displayed

  • Show the bonus ball visually distinct from the base numbers
  • Label multiplier fields clearly
  • Keep the display consistent across games
  • Use supporting guides for visitors who need more context

Design implication

The current results cards separate the special ball visually, which keeps Powerball and Mega Millions easy to scan at a glance.
